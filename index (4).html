Pieces for change

Communities facing deprivation shape the policies that affect them

We surface the untapped potential in the UK's most deprived areas and create pathways for that voice to reach the people who make decisions.

Why We Exist

People in deprived communities know what's broken. They rarely get asked what it is. Even more rarely do they get heard by the people who can fix it.

Mosaic CIC changes that. We research the barriers keeping people excluded from opportunity. We turn that research into evidence policymakers can't ignore. And we make sure communities have a seat at the table when decisions get made.

Because people closest to the problem are closest to the solution, but furthest from power.

What We Do

  • Research with communities. We start with lived experience. We work with communities, not at them. That becomes rigorous research, the kind that shifts policy and employer practice.
  • Build networks that matter. Research alone is just paper. We bring together charities, employers, and decision-makers and hold them accountable to act on what the evidence shows.
  • Advocate for change. We turn evidence into policy recommendations and employer guidance. We don't just document problems. We drive solutions.

Our First Campaign

We're tackling DBS disclosure and unfair employer exclusion, a barrier affecting thousands across the North West. People who've turned their lives around are being locked out of work because of how employers interpret criminal records.

This is our entry point. Our mission is wider: tackling the barriers that drive deprivation across income, employment, education, health, housing, and environment.
